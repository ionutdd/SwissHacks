# Signal: Transactions Involving Sanctioned Addresses  
**Priority:** High  
**Description:**  
Detect transactions where funds are sent to or received from **sanctioned entities** (e.g., OFAC-listed individuals, 
states, or organizations).  
**Detection Methods:**  
- Cross-reference transaction addresses with **sanction lists** (e.g., OFAC, EU sanctions).  
- Monitor for **high-risk wallets** linked to sanctioned entities.  
- Flag transactions with **unusual volume** or **pattern** (e.g., large transfers to offshore accounts).  
**Real-World Example:**  
- **Bitfinex Hack (2021):** Bitfinex was fined $1.2M for failing to block transactions involving sanctioned entities.  

**Notes:**  
- Use blockchain analytics tools (e.g., Chainalysis, Elliptic) for real-time monitoring.  
- Integrate with **regulatory APIs** for automated sanctions list checks.
