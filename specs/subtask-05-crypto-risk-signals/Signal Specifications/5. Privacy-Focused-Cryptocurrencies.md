# Signal: Privacy-Focused Cryptocurrencies  
**Priority:** High  
**Description:**  
Detect transactions involving **privacy coins** (e.g., Monero, Zcash) that obscure sender/receiver identities.  
**Detection Methods:**  
- Flag **privacy coin addresses** (e.g., Monero’s ring signatures).  
- Monitor **mixing services** (e.g., Wasabi Wallet).  
- Use **on-chain analysis** to detect anonymization techniques.  
**Real-World Example:**  
- **Monero Laundering (2016):** Hackers used Monero to launder $1.3M in stolen Bitcoin.  
**Notes:**  
- Use **privacy coin-specific tools** (e.g., Mempool, PrivacyTools).  
- Prioritize **KYC checks** for users of privacy coins.
