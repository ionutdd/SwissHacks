# Signal: Use of Cryptocurrency Mixers  
**Priority:** High  
**Description:**  
Identify transactions that route through **mixers** (e.g., Tumbling Services) to obscure the origin of funds.  
**Detection Methods:**  
- Analyze **transaction patterns** (e.g., multiple small transfers to a mixer address).  
- Monitor **mixer-specific addresses** (e.g., Bitmixer, Hush).  
- Flag **high-risk protocols** (e.g., privacy coins like Monero).  
**Real-World Example:**  
- **Bitfinex Hack (2016):** Hackers used mixers to launder $65M in stolen Bitcoin.  
**Notes:**  
- Use **on-chain analysis** to detect mixer activity.  
- Combine with **off-chain data** (e.g., mixer logs) for accurate detection.
