# Case Study: Ronin Bridge Hack (2022)  
**Signal:** DeFi Platforms for Illicit Activity  
**Summary:**  
DeFi protocols were exploited to steal $625M.  
**Key Details:**  
- The bridge was compromised through validator and protocol weaknesses.  
- The exploit demonstrated how DeFi infrastructure can be abused at scale.  
**Lessons:**  
- Prioritize **DeFi security audits**.  
- Use on-chain analytics to detect **synthetic asset and bridge abuse**.
