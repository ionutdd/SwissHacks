# Case Study: Monero Laundering (2016)  
**Signal:** Privacy-Focused Cryptocurrencies  
**Summary:**  
Hackers used Monero to launder $1.3M in stolen Bitcoin.  
**Key Details:**  
- Privacy features made tracing the funds difficult.  
- The case illustrated how privacy coins can be used to obscure provenance.  
**Lessons:**  
- Flag **privacy coin transactions** for enhanced review.  
- Use specialized tooling for **anonymization detection**.
