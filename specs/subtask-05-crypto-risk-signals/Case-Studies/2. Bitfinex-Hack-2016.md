# Case Study: Bitfinex Hack (2016)  
**Signal:** Use of Cryptocurrency Mixers  
**Summary:**  
Hackers used mixers to launder $65M in stolen Bitcoin.  
**Key Details:**  
- The attack exploited mixer services to obscure transaction origins.  
- The breach underscored the risks of privacy coins in illicit activity.  
**Lessons:**  
- Monitor **mixer-specific addresses**.  
- Combine with **privacy coin analysis tools**.
