# Case Study: FTX Collapse (2022)  
**Signal:** Wallets Linked to High-Risk Entities  
**Summary:**  
High-risk entities were linked to illicit fund transfers.  
**Key Details:**  
- Wallet relationships exposed compliance and governance issues.  
- The event reinforced the need for better wallet risk attribution.  
**Lessons:**  
- Use **blacklist databases** and compliance APIs.  
- Monitor wallet behavior for **unusual network activity**.
