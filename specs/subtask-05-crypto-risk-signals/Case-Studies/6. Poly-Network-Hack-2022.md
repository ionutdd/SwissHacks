# Case Study: Poly Network Hack (2022)  
**Signal:** Smart-Contract-Exploits  
**Summary:**  
An exploit in smart contracts led to $600M theft.  
**Key Details:**  
- The attack leveraged contract logic weaknesses.  
- It showed the impact of poorly secured cross-chain DeFi infrastructure.  
**Lessons:**  
- Monitor **unusual contract interactions** and flash-loan patterns.  
- Strengthen **security audits** for critical contracts.
