# Case Study: Terra/Luna Collapse (2022)  
**Signal:** Unusual Transaction Volumes  
**Summary:**  
Unusual volume spikes in UST led to a $40B collapse.  
**Key Details:**  
- Rapid shifts in transaction patterns signaled instability.  
- The collapse highlighted how abnormal on-chain volume can precede systemic failure.  
**Lessons:**  
- Track **volume anomalies** and wallet activity.  
- Combine on-chain monitoring with **external sentiment signals**.
