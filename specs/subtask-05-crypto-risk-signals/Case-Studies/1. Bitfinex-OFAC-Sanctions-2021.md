# Case Study: Bitfinex OFAC Sanctions (2021)  
**Signal:** Transactions Involving Sanctioned Addresses  
**Summary:**  
Bitfinex was fined $1.2M for failing to block transactions involving sanctioned entities.  
**Key Details:**  
- The platform allowed users to transfer funds to OFAC-listed individuals.  
- The violation highlighted the need for real-time sanction list checks.  
**Lessons:**  
- Integrate **regulatory APIs** for automated monitoring.  
- Use **blockchain analytics tools** for compliance.
